why book is better than television? By Rebin Reji Vazhavilayil

• Imagination: Books allow readers to use their imagination to visualize the story, characters, and setting, making the reading experience more personal and immersive.

• Depth and detail: Books often provide a level of depth and detail that can be difficult to achieve in other forms of media, giving readers a more complete understanding of the story and characters.

• education: Reading books has been shown to improve vocabulary, concentration, and empathy, making it a great educational tool.

• Quiet time: Reading books can provide a quiet, peaceful escape from the noise and distractions of daily life.

• Affordable entertainment: Books are a relatively inexpensive form of entertainment, especially compared to some other forms of media.

• Portability: Books are portable and can be taken anywhere, making them a great form of entertainment on the go.

• Longevity: Books can last for years or even decades, providing multiple opportunities for re-reading and enjoyment.
